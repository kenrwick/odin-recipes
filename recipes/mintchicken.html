Mint Chicken

Oven Roasted Asparagus

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. Toss with the flour just to coat, and shake off excess.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, cover and steam for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
